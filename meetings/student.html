Student Meetings

  1. It is expected that the group will setup and schedule weekly meetings
  2. Instructor is invited to meeting prior to meeting time, but attendance is optional
  3. Team Meetings should take place every week, scheduled based on majority attendance
  4. If an individual(s) cannot make the meeting then the Scrum Master will bring them up to speed
  5. All meetings should have notes taken and shared with the group + instructor afterwards
  6. Meetings should take place on either Zoom or Slack Video Call
